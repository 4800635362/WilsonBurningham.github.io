The Story of Mrs. Danks

Mrs. Mary Danks started baking desserts back in 1985. She started out running a local bake sale out of her house, but quickly realized her desserts were an instant hit! She purchased a small store front on Cherry Street, and expanded her menu to include a bunch of new items. Three years later, we've expanded to four store locations and counting!
